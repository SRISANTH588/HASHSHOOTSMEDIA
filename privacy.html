What Information Do We Collect?

Profile Information

We retain your name, email address, contact number, and postal address. This includes customer profiles, usage preferences, company name, job title, account number, customer identifier, or additional information that relates to you as a customer of OG Shoots.

Transactions and Commercial Information

We do not collect or store payment card data, financial account information, or other financial identifiers. We may keep non-financial transaction metadata (such as confirmation references) only when necessary to deliver the requested service.

User-Generated Content

This includes browser and device data, internet or network activity, data collected through cookies, pixel tags, and other technologies. It also includes geolocation information such as your precise location, city, state, or country if you have geolocation enabled when using the applicable application.

Technical, Electronic and Localisation Information

This includes information captured on audio or video recording, such as if a telephone or video call is recorded. It could include personal information related to your interactions with us to manage your profile, provide relevant customer service, establish communications, conduct our business securely, and optimise our website.
