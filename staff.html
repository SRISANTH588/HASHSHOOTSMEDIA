Staff Terms & Conditions

You must comply with all the following requirements before marking yourself as arrived. Non-compliance may result in shoot cancellation and loss of payment.

ID Card — Mandatory

You must carry your official OG Shoots ID card at all times during the shoot. The client has the right to verify your identity before the session begins. Shooting without an ID card is strictly prohibited.

OG Shoots T-Shirt — Mandatory

You must wear the official OG Shoots branded T-shirt during every shoot. This represents our brand and builds client trust. Arriving without the T-shirt may result in the shoot being cancelled.

Proper Footwear — Mandatory

You must wear clean, formal or smart-casual shoes. Slippers, flip-flops, or torn footwear are not allowed. Proper footwear is part of the professional dress code required at all OG Shoots events.

Dress Code Photo — Required After Event

After attending the event, you must upload a photo of yourself in full dress code (ID card visible, T-shirt, and shoes). This photo is submitted as proof of compliance and is reviewed by the admin before payment is released.

Collect Event Feedback — Required

You must collect feedback from the client at the event location before leaving. Use the “Collect Event Feedback” button after marking arrived. This feedback is submitted to the admin and affects your performance rating.

Professional Conduct

Maintain respectful, professional behaviour at all times. Do not discuss pricing, personal matters, or OG Shoots internal operations with clients. Any misconduct will result in immediate termination and forfeiture of payment.
