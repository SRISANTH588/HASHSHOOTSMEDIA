Terms & Conditions

Please read before purchasing a plan.

Payment Terms
Payment is made one-time upfront for the full contract duration before work begins.

• Basic Plan — ₹14,000 for 6 months
• Standard Plan — ₹22,000 for 6 months
• Premium Plan — ₹40,000 for 3 months

All prices are exclusive of GST (18%). GST will be added at checkout.
Contract Duration
• Basic & Standard plans run for 6 months from the date of payment.
• Premium plan runs for 3 months from the date of payment.

The contract period begins on the date full payment is received and confirmed by OG Shoots.
Cancellation Policy
• Cancellations are accepted within 48 hours of payment for a full refund, provided no work has commenced.
• Once the contract has started (shoot scheduled or content work begun), no refund will be issued.
• OG Shoots reserves the right to terminate the contract if the client is unresponsive for more than 14 consecutive days.
Content Ownership
• All content created under this plan becomes the property of the client upon full payment.
• OG Shoots retains the right to use the content in its portfolio and marketing materials unless the client requests otherwise in writing.
• Raw footage and unedited files are not included unless agreed separately.
Deliverables & Timelines
• Edited reels are delivered within 3–5 business days after each shoot.
• Each reel includes 2 rounds of revisions. Additional revisions are chargeable.
• Shoot dates are scheduled mutually and must be confirmed at least 3 days in advance.
• Delays caused by the client (unavailability, location issues) do not extend the contract period.
