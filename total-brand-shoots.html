Payment Terms
  • All plans require a single one-time payment upfront before the service begins.

  • Standard Plan — ₹22,000 one-time for 6 months of service.
  • Premium Plan — ₹40,000 one-time for 3 months of service.
  • All prices are exclusive of GST. Applicable taxes will be added at checkout.
  • Service begins only after full payment confirmation.
Contract Duration
  • Basic and Standard plans are valid for 6 months from the start date.
  • Premium plan is valid for 3 months from the start date.
  • The contract start date will be confirmed within 48 hours of payment.
