Our Story

Born from a Simple Idea

OG Shoots was born from a simple idea — what if getting a professional reel was as easy as ordering food? We saw creators struggling to find affordable, fast, and reliable content creators. So we built a platform that connects you with trained, certified content makers who arrive at your location and deliver edited content on the spot.

Our Mission

To democratize content creation by making professional reel-making accessible, affordable, and instant for everyone — from solo creators to large brands.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted on-demand content creation platform — where every creator, brand, and business can get world-class reels in minutes.
