Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2025  ·  Effective immediately

This Cookie Policy explains how OG Shoots Media uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. By using our site, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy.

1

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you use the site. Cookies cannot run programs or deliver viruses to your device.

2

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration
hs_user Essential Keeps you logged in to your customer account Session
hs_staff_id Essential Identifies staff members for the staff portal Persistent
hs_razorpay Functional Stores payment configuration for checkout Session
hs_cities Functional Caches available service cities for faster loading 7 days
hs_customer_wallet Functional Stores your wallet balance locally for display Session
_ga, _gid Analytics Google Analytics — helps us understand site usage 2 years / 24 hrs

We primarily use localStorage (a browser storage mechanism similar to cookies) to store your preferences and session data. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to third parties except as described in our Privacy Policy.

3

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for the website to function. They enable core features like login, booking, and payment. You cannot opt out of these cookies while using our services.

4

Functional Cookies

Functional cookies allow us to remember your preferences and provide enhanced features. For example, remembering your city selection or wallet balance. Disabling these may affect your experience.

5

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics tools to understand how visitors interact with our website. This helps us improve our services. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymised — we cannot identify individual users from this data.

We may use Google Analytics for this purpose. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

6

Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Cookies and site data

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website and your ability to use certain features.
